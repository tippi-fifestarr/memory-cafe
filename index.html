Welcome to Our Joyful Space

A place where understanding meets friendship

Please join us at the Delco Memory Cafe, our joyful space specifically for people living with memory loss and their care partners! Connect, relax and enjoy engaging activities in a supportive, judgment-free environment.

You will be among friends who are experiencing similar journeys, sharing stories, doing arts and crafts, playing games, listening to music, and learning from local speakers.

Our Typical Gathering:

  • We begin with warm greetings and easy conversation while snacking on delicious baked goods.
  • Then, we spend time together on a light-hearted activity.
  • We end with a few more minutes of social banter and fun.
